# Experience

## Returning Checkout

Customers reach the payment step and see their saved payment methods above the new-card form. One saved method can be selected at a time.

## Saving a New Method

Customers can opt in to save a new card while entering payment details. The opt-in copy should be clear that the method will be available for future purchases.

## Managing Methods

Customers can open account settings, review saved methods, and remove methods they no longer want.
